The Vault is located in the Iconic Mid Century Modern Building originally known as 1st National Auto Bank on the North West corner of 7th and Cincinnatti. The front door is in the center of the square block of 6th, 7th, Boston and Cincinnati.

FREE Parking!
Free parking is available in the lots surrounding The Vault. There is also a ramp to 2nd Level Parking off of 7th Street.
